RuntzCoin as a Dispensary Coin

RuntzCoin is currently working on becoming the stable coin used in dispensaries all over the world. Anyone who has experienced going to the dispensary understands the struggle. You are being limited to cash, using an ATM, whether cash or cashless, while the ongoing struggle of card networks distancing themselves from dispensaries as a general practice continues. Technicalities still exist between legalized states and areas and still not having access to merchant codes or other difficulties that arise from attempting a transaction using any type of credit or debit card. This is where RuntzCoin enters. We plan to leverage our coin as a bridge between the two opportunities. The dispensary's limitation of what tender it can accept and the consumer having to resort to cash transactions or using an ATM in a digital age and economy. Here we are entering into a new age of digital currency and knocking on the front door of the Metaverse, a literal virtual world taking life and shape before us and we have people having to resort to an ATM in a dispensary.???
